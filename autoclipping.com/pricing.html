SUBSCRIPTION DETAILS

Monthly subscription - the payment is automatically debited and additional clipping credits added in every 30 days.

The subscription can be cancelled at any time. Unused clipping credits will not be refunded and remaning credits will be lost, the forthcoming monthly payment will be cancelled.

The subscription pack can be upgraded at any time. When you upgrade the pack, the subscription charge has to be paid immediately and the additional clipping credits will be added to your unused credits. The next payment will be due 30 days from the time of upgrade.

You can downgrade your subscription plan any time. We'll debit the new monthly subscription plan fee on the date of the downgrade. The next invoice will occur 30 days from the subscription plan downgrade date.

Unused credits roll over for 5 months. After 5 months have passed the monthly subscription will be charged but no more credits will be accumulated. Example: 1000 images subscription roll over limit = 5000 images.

When you run out of subscription credits you can buy more credits using the pay-as-you go option or you can upgrade your plan.

Your credit is used when you download the clipped image. The same image can be re-edited and downloaded without having to use more credits. If you upload the same image again it counts separately as a new image and a credit is used when you download it. NB! With Bulk and API packages credits are used up as images are processed (automatic background removal). Bulk and API packages allow free and unlimited downloads for images processed via the BULK user interface or API.

Your subscription pack is automatically billed and runs until you cancel it.

Subscription fees are non-refundable.

Your subscription pack is personal and cannot be shared with third parties.

Unused Pay-as-you-go credits are valid for 90 days from last Pay-as-you go credit purchase.

Unused subscription plan credits are valid until subscription cancellation date.
